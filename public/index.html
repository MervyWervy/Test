How to Play Educate

Step 1: The Teacher Creates a Room

One person clicks Create New Game to become the Teacher. The Teacher acts as the Game Master! They don't answer questions; instead, they choose the number of rounds, Make questions, control the pace of the game, and judge the answers.

Step 2: Students Join

Other Students click Join Existing Room and enter the 4-digit code given by the Host.

Step 3: Answer the Question

Each round shows a bizarre built-in trivia question. (If the Teacher made their own questions, then those questions are prioritized) Students must invent a fake answer that sounds completely believable to trick their friends.

Step 4: The Teacher Reviews Answers

Before voting begins, the Teacher gets to review everyone's fake answers. If an answer is too lazy, inappropriate, or maybe close to the real answer, the Host can ❌ Reject it! If your answer is rejected, you will receive an alert and must type a new one.

Step 5: Voting & The Teacher's Favorite

All fake answers and the real answer are mixed together on the screen. Players vote for the answer they think is the truth. Meanwhile, the Teacher gets to award a special 🌟 Favorite bonus to the funniest or most creative fake answer! (The Teacher can also avoid awarding by not choosing a fake answer)

Score Points

  • 100 points for voting for the true answer.
  • 50 points for every Student you fool into picking your fake answer.
  • 50 bonus points if the Teacher selects your Fake answer as their favorite!
  • 0 points if you run out of time.

Step 6: Final Results

At the end of the game, the Teacher will reveal the final scoreboard and special awards, including:

  • - Best Fake Artist (Most people fooled)
  • - Smartest Student (Most correct answers)
  • - Most Timeouts (Most no answer submitted)
  • - Champion (Highest overall score)
