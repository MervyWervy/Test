How to Play Educate

Step 1: One Player Creates a Room (The Host)

One player must click Create Room. This player becomes the Host. The host will receive a 4-digit room code.

Step 2: Other Players Join

Other players click Join Room and enter the room code given by the host. All players must enter their name before joining.

Step 3: The Host Starts the Game

When everyone has joined, the host presses the Start Game button. The game will begin for all players at the same time.

Step 4: Answer the Question

Each round shows a question that has a real answer. Players must create a fake answer that sounds believable. Try to trick other players!

Step 5: Vote for the Correct Answer

After everyone submits their fake answer, all answers are mixed together. Players must choose the answer they believe is the real one.

Step 6: Score Points

  • 100 points for choosing the correct answer
  • 50 points when another player chooses your fake answer
  • 0 points if time runs out

Step 7: Multiple Rounds

The game continues for several rounds. Each round has a new question.

Step 8: Final Results

At the end of the game, scores are shown along with special awards such as:

  • Best Fake Artist
  • Smartest Player
  • Most Timeouts
  • Champion
